With a vision to inspire young minds and professionals, BIT Lalpur Campus Ranchi, was started initially with part-time courses in 1998. Currently, Master of Business Administration (MBA), Master of Computer Application (MCA), Bachelor ofBusiness Administration (BBA), Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) programs, and Ph.D. are being offered by the institute.


Academic delivery and learning involve the adoption of a holistic approach to education leading to the overall development of students. The exclusivity of classroom teaching with a blend of the latest technology in educational practicemakes our pedagogy very unique. The key emphasis is on experiential learning with creativity and our students are encouraged to participate in various competitions across all platforms. All-round development of students with student societies and interestgroups indulging in round the year versatile activities. The institute provides a conducive atmosphere for in-depth inquiry into learning, adapting, and evolving.


The focus is on developing innovative thinking among students and nurturing next-generation leaders with rigor based on industry-driven curriculum and the latest pedagogy. We strive to ensure that our studentsdevelop the right skills required to sustain and grow in any organization. With the ultimate aim of creating smart managers and business leaders of tomorrow, emphasis is upon instilling and inculcating a winning spirit.


Students get hands-on real-time exposure to the technical cum professional environment through various short research assignments followed by internship programs in leading MNC organizations.
