BIT Lalpur

With a vision to inspire young minds and professionals, BIT Lalpur Campus Ranchi, was started initially with part-time courses in 1998. Currently, Master of Business Administration (MBA), Master of Computer Application (MCA), Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) programs, and Ph.D. are being offered by the institute.

Academic delivery and learning involve the adoption of a holistic approach to education leading to the overall development of students. The exclusivity of classroom teaching with a blend of the latest technology in educational practice makes our pedagogy very unique. The key emphasis is on experiential learning with creativity and our students are encouraged to participate in various competitions across all platforms. All-round development of students with student societies and interest groups indulging in round the year versatile activities. The institute provides a conducive atmosphere for in-depth inquiry into learning, adapting, and evolving.

The focus is on developing innovative thinking among students and nurturing next-generation leaders with rigor based on industry-driven curriculum and the latest pedagogy. We strive to ensure that our students develop the right skills required to sustain and grow in any organization. With the ultimate aim of creating smart managers and business leaders of tomorrow, emphasis is upon instilling and inculcating a winning spirit.

Students get hands-on real-time exposure to the technical cum professional environment through various short research assignments followed by internship programs in leading MNC organizations.


Some highlights:

  • Full-time/ Part-time Ph.D. programs are being offered in the disciplines of Management and Computer Science.
  • Two years duration full-time Master of Business Administration (MBA) and two years full-time Master of Computer Application (MCA) courses are offered.
  • Three years duration Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) and Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) Courses are offered.


Thus, BIT Lalpur Ranchi campus prides itself in its unique pedagogy and latest curriculum along with a value-based holistic approach to learning in a conducive environment. Our programs help our students to realize their dreams and aspirations of the corporate world and contribute significantly to society with their quest for excellence.
